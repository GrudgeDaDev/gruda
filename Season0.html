Season 0 Fortune Favor Tribes

Each card in Season 0 belongs to one of four tribes, each with unique effects that can shape your strategy. Master these synergies to dominate your opponents!

Iron Will

+1 extra health over normal

Tribe Effect: If there is a Silver card on the field, gain Taunt on summon.

Strategy: Use Iron Will cards to create a defensive wall, protecting your key units.

Blood For Conquest

+1 extra attack over normal

Tribe Effect: If a Red card is on battlefield and you play another Red, it has Haste.

Strategy: Swarm the board with Red cards to overwhelm enemies with fast attacks.

Fabled

Cost one less mana than normal

Tribe Effect: If Purple is in your hand, other Purples cost one less (stacking, min 0).

Strategy: Play Purple cards early to gain tempo and set up powerful combos.

Tribal War

Summon a 1/1 with Taunt when played

Tribe Effect: Give all Orange cards +1 Health on summon if an Orange is on battlefield.

Strategy: Build an army of minions with Orange cards to control the board.

The Story of Ethereal Signatures

In the Season 0 collection, every card you acquire is an Ethereal Signature card, a unique and powerful trait that marks it as one-of-a-kind. Only one copy of each card type across the entire game can possess this trait, with a total mint cap of 100,000 Season 0 cards.

Each Ethereal Signature card features a mythril background that sparkles with an otherworldly glow, along with a radiant green border that signifies its rarity. These cards come with enhanced gameplay effects and stunning visual animations.

Every card in Season 0 also belongs to a tribe, offering unique synergies. Build your deck to maximize these tribal effects and dominate your opponents!

Tribe Strategy Guide

The Season 0 collection introduces the Fortune Favor tribe system as a core gameplay mechanic. Each tribe offers unique strategic advantages that can be combined to create powerful decks.

Iron Will + Blood For Conquest

Combine defensive Silver cards with aggressive Red cards for a balanced strategy. Use Silver's Taunt to protect Red attackers.

Fabled + Tribal War

Use Purple's cost reduction to quickly deploy Orange cards and flood the board with minions that gain +1 Health.

Iron Will + Tribal War

Create an impenetrable wall of defensive minions with Taunt abilities, bolstered by Orange's summoning effects.

Blood For Conquest + Fabled

Deploy high-cost Red attackers earlier with Purple's reduced mana costs, enabling swift and powerful strikes.
