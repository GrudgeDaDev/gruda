Season 0 Rarity Chart

The Story of Rare Rolls

In the Season 0 collection, every card you acquire has a chance to possess special traits that enhance its value and power. These rare traits are determined by the mystical forces of the Grudge universe when cards are minted or discovered in packs.

Each Season 0 card features the special "Season0 Rare Skin" with a mythril background that sparkles with an otherworldly glow. This special skin serves as a canvas for the rare traits that may manifest during the minting process.

When opening a card pack, EVERY card will belong to a tribe. Your deck building strategy should focus on maximizing tribe synergies to dominate your opponents.

Tribe Strategy Guide

The Season 0 collection introduces the Fortune Favor tribe system as a core gameplay mechanic. Each tribe offers unique strategic advantages that can be combined to create powerful decks.

Iron Will + Blood For Conquest

Combine defensive Silver cards with aggressive Red cards for a balanced strategy.

Fabled + Tribal War

Use Purple's cost reduction to quickly deploy Orange cards and flood the board.

Iron Will + Tribal War

Create an impenetrable wall of defensive minions with Taunt abilities.

Blood For Conquest + Fabled

Deploy high-cost attackers earlier with reduced mana costs.
